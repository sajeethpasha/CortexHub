This is a demo of the Microsoft Word–style zoom control. The zoom bar at the bottom lets you scale the view between 50% and 300%.

How to use

  • Click -A to zoom out by 10%.
  • Click A+ to zoom in by 10%.
  • Drag the slider for continuous adjustment.
  • Scroll the mouse wheel over the zoom bar.
  • Click the percentage label to reset to 100%.

Keyboard Shortcuts

While hovering the zoom bar, use Ctrl + and Ctrl − to step zoom in or out. Press Ctrl 0 to reset.

The zoom percentage updates in real time. Minimum zoom is 50%, maximum zoom is 300%, and the default is 100%.
